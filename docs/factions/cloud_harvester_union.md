# The Cloud-Harvester Union (捕云者工会)

## Overview
Based in the **Foundation (根基)** and **Mid-Ring (烟火)** districts, the Union is responsible for the city's survival. They manage the massive **Cloud-Catching Towers (捕云塔群)** that extract water and Ether from the atmosphere. Without their labor, the city would literally run dry and fall from the sky.

## Key Figures
*   **Aria (艾莉亚)**: While primarily a merchant, her control over the "Nectar" (processed Ether/Water) makes her the de facto financier and political voice of the Union in the upper rings.
*   **Foreman Harl (Proposed Entity)**: A veteran of the Sky-Wells who oversees the day-to-day operations of the Foundation's machinery.

## Objectives
*   **Resource Sovereignty**: Ensuring that those who harvest the resources get a fair share of the energy and happiness metrics.
*   **Infrastructure Integrity**: Keeping the **Tian-Shu Engine Room (天枢引擎房)** running despite the increasing frequency of "Gravity Pulses."
*   **Mid-Ring Prosperity**: Supporting the vibrant culture and modular greenery of the **Mid-Ring (烟火)**.

## Territory
Their power base is the **Foundation** district, particularly the industrial zones and the **Siphon Park (虹吸公园)** in the Mid-Ring where they distribute their goods.

## Relation to Other Factions
*   **The Apex Council**: A relationship of mutual dependency and mutual distrust. They frequently negotiate for better equipment and lower altitude quotas.
*   **The Deep-Dwellers**: Many Union workers have relatives among the Deep-Dwellers or secretly provide them with salvaged parts from the **Scrap Metal Recycling Station (废金属回收站)**.
