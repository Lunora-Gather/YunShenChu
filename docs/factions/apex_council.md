# The Apex Council (凌霄高层议会)

## Overview
The Apex Council is the supreme governing body of "Yun Shen Chu" (云深处). Operating from the highest towers in the **Apex (凌霄)** district, they are the architects of the city's laws and the guardians of the **Observer Protocol**. Their visual style is defined by high-transparency glass and silver accents, reflecting their ideal of "transcendental clarity."

## Key Figures
*   **Elder Kael (凯尔长老)**: The High Council Member and Keeper of the Archives. He is the stabilizing force of the council, prioritizing the city's altitude and structural integrity above all else.
*   **Lin Mo (林墨)**: The Chief Architect. While not a politician, his seat as a permanent consultant gives him immense power over urban development and resource allocation.

## Objectives
*   **The Great Ascension**: To continue raising the city's altitude using Ether efficiency breakthroughs.
*   **Stability**: Maintaining the delicate balance of the Gravity Cores and ensuring the lower districts remain productive.
*   **Information Control**: Strictly regulating the historical archives to prevent "misinterpretations" of the city's origin.

## Territory
The council's influence is absolute in the **Apex** district, particularly around the **Administrative Hub (云端枢纽)** and the **Star Hall (星辰礼堂)**.

## Relation to Other Factions
*   **Cloud-Harvester Union**: Views them as essential but unruly labor. Maintains a tense relationship mediated by Aria's commerce.
*   **The Deep-Dwellers**: Officially considers them a "myth" or "unauthorized residents," though they are privately monitored by the council's security forces.
