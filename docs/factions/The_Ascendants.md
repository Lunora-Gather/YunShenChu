# 飞升教派 (The Ascendants)

> "The Gravity Cores do not just lift our city; they lift our souls. We are the chosen, ascending away from the gravity of our sins." 
> — *High Prophet of the Ascendants*

## Overview
**The Ascendants** (飞升教派) is a prominent and increasingly influential cult within *YunShenChu*. To the average citizen, the Gravity Cores are simply marvels of engineering. To the Ascendants, they are divine artifacts, the beating hearts of a higher consciousness that has chosen humanity to rise above the earthly plane.

## Beliefs & Practices

| Doctrine | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **The Holy Resonance** | They believe that the humming of the Gravity Cores is a language. Monks of the order spend years meditating near the engine housings to translate these vibrations into "divine algorithms." |
| **The Sacred Ascent** | Gravity is viewed as a metaphor for worldly attachment and sin. The higher one's physical altitude, the purer their spirit. |
| **The Final Collapse** | A prophecy stating that one day the Cores will reverse their polarity, pulling only the worthy into a higher dimension of pure energy, while dropping the unworthy into the Abyss. |

## Organizational Structure
*   **The Prime Vector (总极向):** The leader of the cult, said to be able to manually adjust the ethereal flow with their mind.
*   **Acoustic Priests (回音祭司):** The inner circle who perform the daily tuning rituals. They wear tuning-fork-shaped headgear.
*   **The Weightless (无重者):** The devout followers who undergo extreme diets and physical modifications to reduce their body weight, striving to be closer to "true floatation."
