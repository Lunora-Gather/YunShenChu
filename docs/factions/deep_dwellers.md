# The Deep-Dwellers (归墟客)

## Overview
The Deep-Dwellers are a decentralized collection of outcasts, rogue engineers, and dreamers who inhabit the "shadows" of the city. They reside in the **Deep Maintenance Wells (深渊维护井)** and the forgotten corners of the **Foundation (根基)**. They are the only ones who dare to look "below the clouds."

## Key Figures
*   **Jax (杰克斯)**: The face of the resistance. His ability to repair "unfixable" tech and his refusal to acknowledge Council authority makes him a hero to the marginalized.
*   **Sela (塞拉) [Secret Sympathizer]**: From her high observation tower, she provides coded gravity data to the Deep-Dwellers, helping them navigate the dangerous "low-gravity" zones.

## Objectives
*   **The Search for Island 14**: To find the legendary "Gui-Xu" (归墟) island, which they believe holds the secret to a life free from Council control.
*   **Gravity Truth**: To uncover the true nature of the "Gravity Pulses" and what the Council is hiding regarding the core's stability.
*   **Horizontal Freedom**: Opposing the rigid vertical hierarchy of the city.

## Territory
They have no official territory, but they are most active in the **Abyss Maintenance Wells (深渊维护井)** and the **Scrap Metal Recycling Station (废金属回收站)**.

## Relation to Other Factions
*   **The Apex Council**: Openly antagonistic. They are often hunted by Council "Observers" for their unauthorized use of Ether.
*   **Shadow Ministry (影之省)**: The true enemy. Deep-Dwellers have encountered "Archivists" while scavenging in the forbidden zones. They know someone is deleting their findings from the Citizen Feed.
*   **Cloud-Harvester Union**: Complicated. Many harvesters are sympathetic to their cause and provide them with "leaked" resources in exchange for Jax's repair services.
