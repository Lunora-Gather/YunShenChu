# [CLASSIFIED] PROJECT: FORBIDDEN DEPTHS
**Security Clearance: Level 5 (Shadow Ministry Only)**
**Subject:** Biological and Phenomenological Observations Below the 4000m Cloud Barrier.

---

## Overview
While the general populace of *YunShenChu* is taught that the world below the clouds is a desolate wasteland of toxic mists and crushing pressure, internal research confirms the existence of a complex, albeit terrifying, ecosystem. These entities are not merely biological; they appear to be composed of condensed Ether and anti-gravity matter.

## Key Entity: The Abyss Whale (Leviathan Class)
Commonly dismissed as "wind whales" in folklore, the **Abyss Whale** (*Balaena Voraginis*) is the apex predator of the lower strata.

*   **Size:** Estimated between 400m and 1.2km in length.
*   **Locomotion:** They do not swim through air so much as they warp the gravity around them. Their movement creates the "Gravity Pulses" often misattributed to engine glitches.
*   **Behavior:** Generally passive, but highly sensitive to the vibration of the Gravity Cores. Recent data suggests they are moving higher into the stratosphere, following the city's energy trail.

## The "Sunken Islands"
Project Forbidden Depths has confirmed that the **Missing Island 14 (归墟)** did not drift away; it was pulled down. Scans reveal the skeletal remains of the island's core at an altitude of -2000m (relative to sea level). It is currently being "nested" in by juvenile Abyss Whales.

## Theoretical Threats: The Pull
The "搏动声" (pulsing sound) recorded in the Gravity Cores matches the heart rate of an Abyss Whale. There is a growing consensus among Ministry scientists that the Cores are not just engines—they are **anchors**. 

**Conclusion:** If the Cores were to fail, *YunShenChu* would not just fall; it would be consumed before it hit the ground.
