# 云端社会志 (Cloud Society)

## 社会阶层 (Social Hierarchy)
“云深处”的社会结构并非基于财富，而是基于对城市重力平衡与生态维持的“共鸣度”：
1.  **引力领航员 (Gravity Navigators)**：负责核心算法与重力平衡的高级技术专家，拥有最高的决策权。
2.  **云际协调员 (Cloud Coordinators)**：管理资源分配、教育与公共事务的中坚力量。
3.  **根基守护者 (Foundation Guardians)**：维持底层工业与重力引擎运作的工程师，深受市民尊敬。
4.  **自由共鸣者 (Free Resonators)**：艺术家、零售商与服务业者，他们为城市注入文化与活力。

## 核心节日 (Core Festivals)
1.  **巅峰黎明节 (The Zenith Dawn)**：每当城市运行轨道达到年度最高点时举行。市民会聚集在露台，用特制的棱镜捕捉第一缕无阻碍的纯净阳光。
2.  **停云祭 (The Still-Cloud Rite)**：纪念重力引擎首次启动成功的日子。全城会进入静默期，关闭所有非必要光源，观察星空。
3.  **色彩季风 (Chromatic Monsoon)**：春季高层气流带来彩色云尘时。人们穿着发光纤维织成的服装，进行跨岛屿的飞行特技表演。

## 云端饮食 (Sky-Cuisine)
*   **以太甘露 (Ether Nectar)**：从云层冷凝水中提纯，加入特种高空藻类发酵，口感清冽且富有能量。
*   **浮空果 (Aero-Fruit)**：在中环模块化温室中培育，内部充满了微型气泡，质地极轻，入口即化。
*   **星光刺身 (Starlight Sashimi)**：利用高空射线对特殊营养基质进行微处理后产生的凝胶状食物，在黑暗中会发出微弱的荧光。

## 心理特质与隐患 (Psychological Traits & Hazards)

> "Don't look down too long. The clouds have a way of looking back, and they are terribly convincing."
> — *Anonymous Foundation Guardian*

### 云渊症候群 (The Cloud Sickness)
**The Cloud Sickness** (云渊症候群) is a uniquely psychological condition affecting the inhabitants of *YunShenChu*, particularly those who spend extended periods gazing at the endless Abyss below the city.

*   **Symptoms**: It begins with a mild vertigo and a creeping sense of detachment from reality. Advanced stages include auditory hallucinations—often described as a low, soothing hum calling from the mist—and an irrational, overwhelming urge to step off the edge of the floating platforms.
*   **The "Call of the Void"**: While the French call it *l'appel du vide*, in YunShenChu, it is considered a physiological reaction to the extreme altitude and the sensory deprivation of the uniform cloud cover beneath them.
*   **Treatment**: Sufferers are temporarily relocated to the inner core of the city, away from any exterior observation decks. Therapy involves exposure to simulated earthly landscapes (soil, grass, solid horizons) and grounding exercises in high-gravity simulation chambers.
